Timofeev, Vahrenwald & Partners contributes to EU-Russia Centre Review

17 June 2010

TV&P contributed a piece to the 14th edition of the EU-Russia Centre Review published Monday 31 May 2010 titled “EU-Russia Legal Co-operation”. The piece titled “A Brief Overview of the Russian Legal System” gives a brief overview of the Russian legal system’s structure and some of its weaknesses. Furthermore, the article gives the reader an insight into the quality of legal counsel in Russia.

The Review was published in the EU capital Brussels and distributed among politicians, academics, and top EU officials interested in Russia and in enhancing closer ties between the EU and Russia. TV&P is proud to contribute with its knowledge to a leading think-tank on EU-Russia affairs. This co-operation shows TV&P ongoing commitment to international co-operation in legal affairs and reflects its expertise as a competent and sound European law firm. The article may be downloaded at official website of the EU-Russia Centre or at the TV&P website.

If you need further information on the subject, contact Kristoffer Svendsen.
